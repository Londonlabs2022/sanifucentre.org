Profile

Sanifu Centre is a Language Centre located in Thika, Kenya. We specialize in offering high-quality foreign language courses, particularly German, French, Spanish and English. Whether you’re interested in studying, living, working in Germany, France,Spain or bridge Cultures and Communication, exam preparation or just for fun, talk to us!
Sanifu Centre aims to bridge the gap and provide valuable resources for those seeking to engage with the German language and culture. You can find more information about Sanifu Centre on their Facebook page, X, Instagram or explore our content on Instagram.
